About Us

Mission

To craft exceptional savory pies with premium ingredients and bold flavor, delivering a satisfying, protein-forward handheld experience that is accessible seamlessly in modern American life.

Vision

To make savory pies a beloved American staple, served hot and fresh in cafés and food trucks and stocked in grocery freezers across the country.

Story

We started Hungry Pebble because America deserves a better savory pie: something warm, satisfying, and easy to enjoy anywhere. We’re bringing a comfort-food staple to more people, one golden crust at a time.
